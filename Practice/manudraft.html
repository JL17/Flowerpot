1999, Round 2, Pick: 57

By: Jason Lee

As the title precludes, in the 1999 NBA Draft, every GM not named R.C. Buford passed on Argentinian phenom, Emanuel David "Manu" Ginobili Maccari. Let's just validate this move as the greatest value pick of all time as the 2nd round guard went on to acquire the following accolades:
4 NBA championships, 2 NBA All-Star appearances, 2 All-NBA Third Team nods, and the NBA Sixth Man of the Year Award in 2008.
